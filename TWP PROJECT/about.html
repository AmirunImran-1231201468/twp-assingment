Our Mission

To deliver the most satisfying and joy-filled milkshakes to every student’s doorstep with speed, quality, and a smile.

Our Vision

To become the most loved and convenient milkshake brand on campus—where every sip brings happiness and every order feels personal.

Our Story

Founded by a group of MMU students with a love for creamy indulgence, Milkshake Haven started as a small dorm-room idea. What began as midnight milkshake runs has grown into a full-fledged delivery system serving our campus with thick, delicious flavors! Our secret? Real ingredients, local support, and endless passion for frozen goodness.
